Photography

Ever since middle school, I've always been fascinated by the tech in cameras, which drove me to learn how they worked. With this, I also developed my creative eye, and became engrossed in the world of photogrpahy.

As I became more proficient and efficient with my camera, I reached out to friends and family to provide an affordable freelance photography solution, and grew my experience in drone photography, as well as standard portraiture.

Soon after this, I became more interested in cinema, specifically the technical process of filmaking, from storyboarding, filming, and editing. I became head of media for my robotics team, producing multiple videos which eventually helped the team win a Chairman's award, considered the most prestigious award at a regional event. To further my invovement with cinematography, I worked on an Independent Study Project focusing on producing two mini-documentaries, over topics me and my partner found interesting.
